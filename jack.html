Jack Tequila (AKA: Jaqueline)

Jack was rescued in 2019, he was only two years old. He is an extremely loving dog and has a very eminent personality. He is good with kids and gets excited when anyone walks in through the door. He has a lot of traumas from his previous owner (who used to beat him), so he is a little bit tricky to deal with. It took a lot of time and patience for him to feel comfortable around our family. Jack is the family dog. He stays a few days at my parents house, where I live, and a few days at my sister's apartment. We all miss him when he is not home. He is the “xodó” of the family, which in Portuguese means “one or that which, due to emotional ties, is placed in a favorable position.”
