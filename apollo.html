Apollo (AKA: Frajola)

Apollo is the best thing that happened to me. He is my cat and mine only, I am fully responsible for him and I love it. He brings light to my life. I call him “the love of my life, my reason to leave” everytime I talk to him. He is sweet and extremely chill (except during zoomies sessions). I love him with all my heart. After I got him I started to question myself, “how did I live so long without this little creature?”. Apollo is only a year old but I feel like I have had him for much longer.
